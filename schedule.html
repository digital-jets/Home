Job Expo '36

What's On

Three days of guided tours, roleplay experiences, and live activities across the Smart Home, Neighbourhood, and Hospital of the Future.

🗺️

Guided Tours

All Guided Tours can accommodate a maximum of 15 people, on a first-come-first-served basis. Register your spot through JETS Registration.

🎭

Roleplay

Step into tomorrow's healthcare settings through immersive roleplay scenarios across the Smart Home, Neighbourhood, and Hospital zones. Open to all visitors.

🎤

Activities

Catch the Main Stage programme, join drop-in games, and take part in live interactive challenges spread across the three-day event.
