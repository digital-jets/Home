With Gratitude

Acknowledgements

Job Expo '36 is the result of months of dedicated work by a large community of volunteers, industry professionals, and public sector partners. We extend our sincere thanks to everyone who contributed their time, expertise, and enthusiasm to bringing this event to life.

A special thank you to all exhibiting organisations for their commitment to connecting with the next generation of workers across the Home, Neighbourhood, and Hospital zones. Your presence makes this expo truly meaningful.

We are grateful to our keynote speakers, workshop facilitators, and panel participants for generously sharing their knowledge and experience with our visitors. Your insights will inspire countless career journeys.

Thank you to the venue and catering teams, technical staff, stewards, and all behind-the-scenes contributors whose professionalism ensures a smooth and welcoming experience for every attendee.

Finally, thank you to every visitor who attended Job Expo '36. We hope you leave with new connections, fresh ideas, and the confidence to pursue the career that is right for you.

Interested in sponsoring a future Job Expo event? We welcome enquiries from organisations who share our commitment to careers, community, and opportunity. Contact the organising team to learn more.
