Who We Are

About JETS

Jobs Expo Thirty Six (JETS) is a landmark careers exhibition by Community Health Institutes (CHI), themed Future Workforce for Future Health. It brings together healthcare employers, job seekers, and industry leaders to explore 36 new roles shaping home, community, and hospital care in 2036.

Walk through three immersive zones, try hands-on roleplay experiences, attend the main stage programme, and complete your stamp rally boarding pass. Whether you are a student, a career switcher, or an experienced professional, JETS is your gateway to the future of healthcare.
