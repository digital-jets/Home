Who We Are

About JETS

Jobs Expo '36 (JETS) is a future-focused exhibition that positions healthcare as a future-ready, innovative, and people-centric sector. Designed as a seamless journey across the Smart Home, Neighbourhood, Virtual Care Centre, and Smart Hospital, JETS immerses visitors in the healthcare landscape of 2036 — demonstrating how care will be delivered across interconnected environments, enabled by technology and powered by a transformed workforce.

Visitors follow the continuum of care from prevention to recovery, experiencing how healthcare shifts from episodic, hospital-centric treatment to proactive, community-based, and data-driven care. At every stage, future job roles illustrate how professionals will work across disciplines and environments to coordinate care, enhance patient outcomes, and improve system resilience.

JETS is designed to inspire healthcare professionals, youths, and the general public — showcasing the possibilities of future careers and inviting all stakeholders to co-create the future of healthcare and participate in Singapore's ongoing workforce transformation journey.

Your Journey

How It Works

✅
Step 1
Check-In

Collect your deck box at the entrance, then load up JETS on your mobile — this site is your companion guide through the exhibition.

🔍
Step 2
Seek

Navigate the zones to build your workforce. Explore job roles around the hall — each role on display has a unique trading card for you to collect.

⚡
Step 3
Level-Up

Gather buffs for your workforce. Collect power-up cards by attending CHI Offices' Workshops and by visiting CHI partner booths.

🚀
Step 4
Time to Fly!

Head to the JETS Trading Café to trade cards with other visitors and play the game.
