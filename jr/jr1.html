Nurse Choreographer

Hospital Community Home
← Back to Job Roles

The Nurse Choreographer serves as the single point of oversight for complete patient journeys, providing continuous care from baseline community health through acute episodes and recovery. This role combines clinical triage, anticipatory care planning, and psychosocial expertise to implement family-oriented care, with comprehensive knowledge of care resources across settings. Operating within integrated teamlet models and functioning trans-disciplinarily across multiple providers, the Nurse Choreographer leverages remote monitoring and data integration to coordinate care for assigned patient cohorts, ensuring continuous therapeutic relationships and seamless care transitions.
